GOD OF WAR

Explore the mythos of ancient gods

EXPLORE

Exploration in God of War involves navigating through the expansive, detailed environments of Midgard and other realms. The game encourages players to venture off the beaten path, discover hidden areas, and solve environmental puzzles.

COMBAT: A central gameplay element that involves engaging in battles against a variety of enemies, from common foes to powerful bosses. The combat system is dynamic, combining melee attacks, ranged attacks, and special abilities.

CONNECTIONS

Refer to the relationships and interactions between characters, primarily between Kratos and his son, Atreus. These connections are crucial to the narrative and emotional depth of the game.
