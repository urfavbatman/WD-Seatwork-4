HERO

From magical tacticians to fierce brutes and cunning rogues, Dota 2's hero pool is massive and limitlessly diverse. Unleash incredible abilities and devastating ultimates on your way to victory.

HEROES

Heroes are the playable characters controlled by players throughout a Dota 2 match. Each hero has unique abilities, attributes (such as strength, agility, and intelligence), and roles (like carry, support, initiator, etc.).

MAP

Dota 2 refers to the playing field where matches take place. It consists of three main lanes (top, middle, and bottom) that connect the two team bases. Each lane is populated by defensive structures (towers) and non-playable characters (creeps) that spawn periodically and move towards the enemy base.

ITEMS

Items provide various benefits such as increased attributes (strength, agility, intelligence), enhanced abilities, regeneration of health or mana, and unique active or passive effects.
